Best Cheap VPS for Game Servers (2026)

⏱️ 8 min read 📅 Updated May 2026 💰 Pricing

Picking the wrong VPS is the #1 mistake new game server owners make. High ping, shared CPU cores that throttle under load, or no DDoS protection — it kills the experience for your players.

I've tested all of these personally. Here's the honest breakdown.

What matters for game servers: Single-thread CPU speed (not just core count), low latency to your players, reliable DDoS protection, and enough RAM. Storage is almost never the bottleneck.
